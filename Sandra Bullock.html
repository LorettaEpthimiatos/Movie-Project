SANDRA BULLOCK





Sandra Bullock usually plays the role of a Strong, independent woman, frequently in law enforcement, who learns to let her guard down through love and/or enduring friendship. Some of Bullock’s movies in this role are; The Heat, The Proposal, Miss Congeniality, Two Weeks Notice, Murder By Numbers, 28 Days, Practical Magic, Speed, While You Were Sleeping, Demolition Man. Either Sandra Bullock gets a real power trip from strapping on a handgun holster, or there is something about playing FBI agents and other rough-around-the-edges women that she identifies with. But I’m not complaining because the moments when her character lets her guard down (the Betty White forest scene in The Proposal, for example) are absolutely hilarious.
